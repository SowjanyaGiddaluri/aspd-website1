    • About ASPD

    Over a decade of experience in the hospitality industry.

    Founded in 2014, AMERICAN SKILLS & PERSONALITY DEVELOPMENT began with a small group of students and has since achieved remarkable success by offering international internships in the USA, France, Spain, Mauritius, UAE, and Thailand.

    We aim to equip students with a clear pathway to effective learning while also encouraging them with our supportive presence throughout their journey toward success. In summary, many students perceive the AMERICAN SKILLS & PERSONALITY DEVELOPMENT experience as both innovative and distinctive.

    Our Internship Programs

    The ASPD internship program is designed to offer Hospitality college students the chance to engage in practical training experiences related to their field of study. This program provides students with the opportunity to translate theoretical knowledge into real-world applications, gain valuable career experience, and foster personal development while obtaining an International certificate from a recognized Hotel or Resort. ASPD collaborates with international hospitality internship providers to facilitate practical training opportunities lasting from 3 to 12 months across the globe. This distinctive academic initiative merges classroom education with hands-on work experience. Our Internship Placement Program enables students and emerging professionals to acquire professional skills and broaden their perspectives through on-the-job training at esteemed star Hotels or Resorts in the USA, France, and other countries.

    Global Internship Opportunities

    Gain invaluable hands-on experience through our international internship programs. We connect you with prestigious hotels and resorts worldwide, allowing you to immerse yourself in diverse cultural settings and develop a global perspective that is highly sought after by employers.

    Expert Guidance and Support

    Our seasoned trainers and career advisors are committed to guiding you every step of the way. From resume building to interview preparation, we provide the resources and support you need to make a strong impression on international recruiters.

    Comprehensive Visa Assistance

    Navigating the complexities of visa applications can be daunting. Our Visa Assistance services simplify the process, ensuring that you meet all requirements and facilitating a smooth transition to your international internship or job placement.

    Networking and Professional Growth

    By joining ASPD, you become part of a robust network of hospitality professionals and alumni. We facilitate connections that can lead to mentorship opportunities, industry insights, and potential job offers, allowing you to cultivate relationships that propel your career forward.

    Resume and Interview Preparation

    We understand the importance of a strong resume and effective interview skills in securing job offers. Our specialists work closely with you to craft a compelling resume that highlights your strengths and experiences, while our mock interview sessions equip you with the confidence and skills needed to succeed.
